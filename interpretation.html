Key themes

  • Legal form vs. coercion: Treaties and statutes present procedural fairness while enabling dispossession.
  • Rhetoric as cover: “Benevolent” language framed removal as protective and progressive, prioritizing state growth.
  • Human toll: Family stories document suffering, death, and trauma that legal texts obscure.
